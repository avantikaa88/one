About Us

We are a team of animal lovers dedicated to giving every pet a chance at a happy and healthy life. At Buddy, we connect rescued and abandoned animals with caring individuals who can offer them a safe and loving home. Along with adoption, we also provide reliable veterinary services to ensure that every pet receives the proper care, treatment, and attention they deserve.

Our goal is to make adoption and pet care easy, transparent, and rewarding for both pets and their owners. Every adoption tells a story of hope, healing, and a fresh start.
